How Identity Compromise Occurs

Aadhaar data is often exposed through secondary service providers and sophisticated social engineering.

01 / EXPOSURE
Provider Leaks

Banks, telcos, and utility providers store Aadhaar data. If their cloud buckets are unsecured, your ID and sensitive metadata are leaked to the dark web.

02 / INTERCEPTION
Biometric Cloning

Advanced scammers harvest fingerprint data from public property registries or use specialized gummy-finger tech to bypass AePS authentication.

03 / TAKE OVER
OTP Social Engineering

Criminials call you posing as government officials to trick you into sharing the Aadhaar OTP, allowing them to verify fraudulent transactions in your name.

How to Audit Your Identity

Impact of Identity Theft

A compromised Aadhaar is not just a leak—it is a total compromise of your legal and financial standing.

CRITICAL
AePS Fraud

Attackers use biometric clones to silently withdraw up to ₹10,000 per day from your bank without needing an OTP or Physical Card.

CRITICAL
Ghost Bank Accounts

Your leaked Aadhaar can be used to open fraudulent bank accounts used for money laundering, making you legally liable for their crimes.

HIGH RISK
Illegal Sim Cards

Criminals use your identity for bulk SIM registration. These numbers are then used in terrorism or high-value extortion scams.

CRITICAL
Loan Liabilities

By linking your Aadhaar to fake income documents, fraudsters can obtain high-interest loans in your name, destroying your credit score.

HIGH RISK
Tax Evasion Link

Unauthorized linking of your Aadhaar to malicious PAN cards is used to hide income, leading to severe penalties from tax authorities.

HIGH RISK
Welfare Fraud

Attackers can attempt to hijack your government subsidies or PDS rations by authenticating using cloned identity markers.

Defensive Strategy

Prevention Tips

Enable Biometric Lock

The single most effective defense. Lock your fingerprints via MyAadhaar to disable all biometric authentication.

Use Masked Aadhaar

Download a version of your Aadhaar where only the last 4 digits are visible for general identity verification.

Generate Virtual ID (VID)

Use a temporary VID instead of your permanent Aadhaar number when authenticating with third-party services.

Crisis Response

Mitigation Tips

Call 1947 Immediately

UIDAI's dedicated helpline for reporting unauthorized Aadhaar usage or authentication failures.

Audit Bank Statements

Check for 'AePS Withdrawal' entries. If you find one you didn't authorize, report it to your bank's fraud cell within 24 hours.

Lodge Cyber Grievance

File an official report on the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) with your auth history logs.
