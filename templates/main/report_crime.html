Action Protocol Wizard

1

Secure Your Accounts

Immediately change passwords for compromised accounts. Do not use the same password for multiple services.

2

Block Financial Assets

Contact your bank's 24/7 hotline to freeze credit cards, debit cards, and net banking if financial data is compromised.

3

Preserve Evidence

Take screenshots of emails, texts, websites, and chat histories before deleting anything. Do not confront the attacker.

4

File Official Report

Submit all evidence to cybercrime.gov.in and retain the reference number for future inquiries with local authorities.
